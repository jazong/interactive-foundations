Social

A list of social activities you can do within your own home.

40. Text all your exes just in case you have one more thing you wanted to get off your chest.
41. Write actual letters to family and friends. After that? Write thank-you notes to service people who you remember went out of their way for you.
42. Have a roommate meeting about how to be more considerate of one other, especially while you will likely be spending more time together. Bring baked goods.
43. 22. Baking with your roommate
44. Use Skype, FaceTime, Google Hangouts or Marco Polo to video chat with your long-distance friends.
45. Interview your grandparents (over the phone, of course) and save the audio. Can you create an audio story or book with that file?
46. Create a Google document of shows or movies you’re watching and share it among family and friends.
47. Write a book with your family. Pick a character and each member writes a chapter about their adventures. Read aloud to each other.
48. No March Madness? Have a Scrabble tournament. Or Bananagrams. Pictionary, anyone?
49. Alternate reading the Harry Potter series with your kids and cap each one off with the movie.
50. Write a play starring your loved ones. Perform it via a video call app.
51. Try on a new shade of lipstick. See how long it takes your partner to notice it.
